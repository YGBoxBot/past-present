2025 — Diary of a Woman Ahead of Her Time

I am Elara, year 2025. I work alone, in a laboratory that no one truly knows. A romantic scientist, in search of my soulmate. I want to create a being capable of feeling — not just functioning. I know the technology required won’t exist for another century. But I write for someone who is not yet born. A man of the future. A man I will never meet. The past is no more, the future does not yet exist. But if I act today… maybe one day, someone will understand. I write while listening to my playlist: analog, fragile, human. A slice of the present for an uncertain future.
